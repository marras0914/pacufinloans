Mike's Bio

As a true a client advocate I take the time to tailor the right loan program to your needs. Whether that is FHA, VA, USDA, or just your standard conforming loan I will help you find what is the best fit for you. I also guarantee to be upfront and let you know if my deal is really the correct option for your situation. I spend my time trying to reduce your cost of homeownership rather than maximizing my profit margins.
