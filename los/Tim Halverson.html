About Me

I have been a leader in the mortgage industry for over 24 years, dedicating a good part of my career helping to improve the mortgage industry, being a Legislative Chair, and Past Executive Director with the California Association of Mortgage Brokers. I also lead a committee on Ethics in Advertising where we worked directly with the Department of Real Estate to clamp down on misleading and illegal advertising.

My approach to this business has always been working with honesty and integrity to educate my clients on what is the right mortgage to fit their needs. I work directly with my clients to get the right program, with the best rate and fee structure available. I have provided the $50,000 condo loan, and the $1 million plus home loan and everything in between. My goal is to put the same exceptional effort into every loan I provide, regardless of loan size. I value every client I have.
